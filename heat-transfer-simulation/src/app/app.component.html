How It Works

This simulation calculates the final temperature of water in a storage tank using the following steps:

  • Solar Panel: Collects energy based on efficiency, area, and solar radiation.
  • Pump: Transfers collected energy with a given efficiency.
  • Storage Tank: Calculates the final temperature based on the transferred energy, capacity of the tank, and initial water temperature.

Ensure that all input values are greater than zero and properly entered to get accurate results.
